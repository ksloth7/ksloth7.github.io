K-Pop Demon Hunters

Genre: Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Animation Rating:

Synopsis:

Three K-Pop singers have a hidden secret that they must keep from the world. These superstars lead a double life fo fighting demons. Their next threat is a demonic boyband. This movie is based off of Korean mythology and shamanism "Mugyo". Mugyo are powerful women who use song and dance to power the Honmoon, a spiritual sheild that wards off evil and protects the living.

The Hunger Games: Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes

Genre: Action, Adventure, Science Fiction, Drama Rating:

The Hunger Games: Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes is a prequel to the original Hunger Games trilogy. It follows the story of Coriolanus Snow, a young man from a wealthy family who becomes the mentor of a young girl named Lucy Gray. As the story unfolds, we learn about the origins of the Hunger Games and the political tensions that led to their creation.
